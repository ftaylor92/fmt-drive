OneTap NoteAndroid App


One-Tap Note allows you to create notes with one tap of your android, then just speaking or typing what you want noted. Period. That's It.

It saves your note as a Google Drive Document, so you have all the resources and security of Google™ Drive (to print, copy, share, bookmark, view online, etc.)

You may view your ToDo note from the One-Tap Note app or from creating a short-cut to it on your phone using Google Drive app widget.

You can take notes anytime, even when not connected to internet because all notes taken offline are saved in the app's database and synched to your Google Document ToDo list later.




For Developers:

One-Tap Note android app accesses a Google Script to insert note into a Google Drive document.

Here is the Create Document Script. Here is the Insert Note Script. You can copy this script and modify it to do different things. You can change the One-Tap Note app widgets to use your new Google Script by changing the One-Tap Note app's "Insert Script URL" Preference.

The Google Drive ToDo Document that you create and modify has a unique ID, which you'll need in set in the One-Tap Note app's "Google Drive Document ID" Preference.

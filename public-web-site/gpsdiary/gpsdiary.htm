GPS Diary App


Translate GPS into readable Diary.
  • Great to see where you went and where you visited, shopped and ate on vacation.
  • Compass to Find your Car even if you forgot to "Mark" it!
  • Speed-aware: Knows if you traveled by foot, bike or car.
  • Edit, Save, Import, Export, Cut&Paste between GPX, KML, App, online, notepad and Txt.
  • Great for people who want to keep a Journal but are too lazy to do so.
  • Share your travel details with friends want to know what you did, not just the GPS data.

This app uses Yahoo! Reverse Geocoding API, GeoNames Reverse Geocoding Webservice, Google Reverse GeoCoding API and Google Places API to translate GPS points into readble addresses.
This app's GPS analysis engine is available as an open-source library on sourceforge.

CHANGE ME:Zev Rovine Selections is a boutique wine and spirits import and distribution company that services restaurants, caterers and retailers in New York City and the surrounding area. We work with wines that are produced in France, Italy, Spain, Germany, Argentina, and the United States. 


CHANGE ME:With such a broad group of sources, it is hard to state a congruent mantra or set of technical standards that binds all of our wines. One thing does however seem to reverberate throughout the portfolio, and that is the idea of “sense of place.” Whether a wine hails from the steep slopes of the Mosel, or the arid hills of Ribera del Duero, all of our wines reflect the individual terroir that they hail from. While the winemakers at each property we represent have a different set of philosophies, they all seem to share the idea that the best wines are made in the vineyard, not the winery. They strive to express the pulse of their land through the lens of their wine.


CHANGE ME:At ZRS, our goal is to bring new and exciting wines to a market rife with boring, industrialized wine. Please email or call for our current portfolio of artisan wines.

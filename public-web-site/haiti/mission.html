Mission Statement



The St. Boniface Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helpling the poor and needy in Haiti. Its primary focus is its support of the St. Boniface Medical Clinic in Fond des Blancs, Haiti. It also brings work retreats composed of American citizens to experience Haiti, to bring medicines, and help in the clinic. It continues to raise the living standard of the Fond des Blanc area, by developing nutrition and vaccination progams, and by channeling contributions to the most needy of its residents.
